The Meaning of Pragna

From Sanskrit: प्रज्ञा (Prajñā)

Pragna embodies the essence of transcendent wisdom, intuitive understanding, and profound insight that goes beyond conventional knowledge.

In our context, PragnaAI represents the intelligence that perceives what the eyes cannot see - detecting the subtle molecular signatures of disease before they manifest as physical symptoms.
